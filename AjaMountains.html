About Aja Mountains


The Aja Mountains have been designated as an Important Plant Area and an Important Bird and Biodiversity Area. The region is steeped in ancient history, with inscriptions and petroglyphs that date back thousands of years, making it an important archaeological site in Saudi Arabia.
