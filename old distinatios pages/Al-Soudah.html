About Al-Soudah


Part of Asir National Park, Al-Soudah has long been a cultural hub for the Asir people. Known for its unique architecture and rich traditions, the region has been inhabited for centuries and played a key role in the spice trade routes.



More Information


  • City: Abha
  • Region: South
